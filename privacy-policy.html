VibePet Privacy Policy

Effective June 14, 2026

VibePet is a local-first macOS application. It does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local data

Videos, transparent companion assets, packages, preferences, and household configuration are processed and stored on your Mac. Export occurs only when you choose a destination with the macOS save panel.

Permissions

VibePet does not request camera, microphone, screen recording, Accessibility, location, Contacts, Photos, or Bluetooth access. It accesses files only after you select them with a standard macOS file panel.

Network and tracking

VibePet contains no advertising, analytics, tracking, account, or cloud service. The current version does not send requests to AI providers.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion assistance, open a private-data-free support request at github.com/Mikekeduo/vibepet-support/issues. Do not include personal media or confidential information in a public issue.
