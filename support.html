VibePet Support

Creating a companion

Use a 0.5 to 10 second MOV or MP4 with a solid green or blue background, or an existing HEVC-with-alpha video. Keep the camera fixed and the full body visible.

Privacy

Processing is local. VibePet does not upload companion videos.

Contact

For help, bug reports, or feature requests, visit github.com/Mikekeduo/vibepet-support/issues. Do not attach private pet videos or confidential information to a public issue.
